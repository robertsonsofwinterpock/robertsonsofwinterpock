MOSES M ROBERTSON FARMHOUSE

In July 2003, Ray Robertson, Helen Robertson Creekmore, and her son Rob visited the house bought and expanded by Moses M Robertson prior to the Civil War. Benjamin Moses Robertson and his older sister Judith Anne were born and grew up here. This is the setting of many of the stories in Judith Anne's letter. Judith Ann returned here with her husband and continued to run the farm for a time. This is also where the famous silver spoon - presented to Ingram Robertson Sr.(Benjamin's son) - was discovered in a nearby field where it had been left when the Robertsons had buried their silverware to hide it from the union troops.
